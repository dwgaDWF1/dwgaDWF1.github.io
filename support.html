Frequently Asked Questions
  • How can I become a Moderator?
    Moderator applications open around once a month, and we select about 2-4 applicants every round. Make sure to join our Discord server to be notified once we open them!
  • What does VIP give you?
    The VIP gamepass comes with an exclusive weapon, a chat tag, 100,000 tokens, and 2X map vote!
  • How do I find a code?
    You can find codes on our Discord server, game descriptions, and most commonly, on our Twitter!
  • How do I redeem a code?
    Once you have found a valid/active code, head over to our game and click the Twitter icon. From there, paste in or type in your code and hit redeem. You are done! Enjoy your reward.
If you have any additional questions, feel free to reach out through social media or fill out this form! https://belugagames.io/contact
